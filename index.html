Our Goal

We want all participants of a team to come together as a startup and Make something. All events are interconnected to each other, and add toward the final goal of creating a useful product. About Our Team

At G³ we explore the exciting world of technology together. Through hands-on activities, coding workshops, and creative projects, we learn about programming, digital design, and more. Our club provides a space to collaborate, problem-solve, and unleash our imaginations. Whether we're developing apps, designing websites, or diving into robotics, we're constantly expanding our tech skills and having a blast while doing it!
